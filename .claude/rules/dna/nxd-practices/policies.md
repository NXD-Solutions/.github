# Policies

Policies drive decisions from necessity. Where strands say who we are, policies say what we must hold — regardless of who we are.

A policy may be strand-expressed, externally mandated, or strategically decided. Source is documented per entry; no strand parent is required.

Principles that enforce a policy name it with `Enforces: <policy name>` in their text.

---

## Preserve the ability to change

Vendor, partner, and technology substitution must always be possible without a system rewrite.

**Source:** Sovereign by Design (strand-expressed)
