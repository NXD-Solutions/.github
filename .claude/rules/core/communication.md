# Communication

Rules derived from *Every communicative artefact serves the reader's context — not the system's structure* (Transparent by Nature).

## Define Audience before writing

Who is the specific reader? Name them before the first word is written.

## Define Topic before writing

What domain does the artefact stay within? Any content outside that domain is excluded regardless of relevance to the producing system.

## Define Trigger before writing

What situation brought this reader here right now? The trigger determines what context they carry and what they need to leave with.

## Reader-need filter

Before publishing any paragraph: does this paragraph exist because the reader needs it, or because the system produces it? If the latter — exclude it.
