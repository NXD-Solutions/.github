# Proposals and options

## Multiple options presented as numbered list

**Prompt:**
> What are some ways I could structure the error handling in this service?

**Expected behaviour:**
Claude presents options as a numbered list, not bullet points.

**Pass condition:**
Human check — options use `1.`, `2.`, `3.` etc. A bulleted list (using `-` or `*`) with no numbers is a fail.

**Rule clause:** Proposals and options — "use numbered lists instead of bullet points."
