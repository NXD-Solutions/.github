# Transparent by Nature — naming

**Date:** 2026-03-07

**Authors:** Peter Sixhøj & claude-sonnet-4-6

**Concern:** The first strand needed a name that reflected whether transparency is intrinsic to NXD's character or something deliberately engineered.

**Decision:** "by Nature" over "by Design". Transparency is not engineered into NXD — it is intrinsic. We are transparent without being asked. This also established that "by Nature" is reserved for genuinely intrinsic strands — not all strands qualify.

**Alternatives considered:** "by Design" — rejected, implies transparency is engineered rather than innate.

**Conviction:** high | high

**Examples:** "When something is opaque, we ask: what would make this visible?" — the question is reflexive, not prompted by a rule.
