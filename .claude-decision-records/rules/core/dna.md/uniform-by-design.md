**Date:** 2026-03-07

**Authors:** Peter Sixhøj & claude-sonnet-4-6

**Concern:** Uniform and Lean both touch consistency. The overlap raised the question of whether two strands were justified.

**Decision:** Distinct core values justify two strands. Lean removes what costs without return; Uniform builds consistency as a stable foundation. The test: name what each one does to the system.
