# dna.md strand grouping and ordering rationale

**Decision:** The strands in `dna.md` are organised into three labelled groups with a fixed order. Transparent by Nature stands outside all groups as an ungrouped opener.

**Finding:** The strand names alone do not convey how the strands relate to each other or why one precedes another. Without a record, any future session re-opening order or grouping would re-litigate the same deliberation from scratch.

**Trigger:** Reword of `dna.md` for `thinking-core/` placement required reordering. The new order encodes a causal claim that is not surface-readable.

**Consequence of no record:** Grouping and order treated as arbitrary. Future sessions re-group or re-order without the causal argument, producing drift or redundant deliberation.

**Goal:** Make the structural claim recoverable without re-deliberation.

---

## Rationale

**Transparent by Nature — ungrouped opener**

Transparent by Nature is the enabler of all other strands — without visibility, no other strand can be evaluated, corrected, or improved. It precedes everything and belongs to no group because it is not a practice; it is the condition under which all practices function.

**Three groups follow a causal chain: Thinking → Effective working → Survival**

Think correctly first — finding patterns, treating solutions as starting points — and that produces work that is lean and uniform. Work that is lean and uniform produces systems that are resilient, sovereign, and secure.

**Within Thinking: Evolving before Pattern-seeking**

Evolving by Instinct is the disposition — the commitment to treat every solution as a starting point. Pattern-seeking by Instinct is the instinct that disposition produces. You evolve, therefore you seek patterns.

**Within Survival: Resilient → Sovereign → Secure**

A hardening sequence. Resilience is the base condition (keeps working), Sovereignty is the ownership condition (survives dependency failure), Security is the closure condition (door stays closed). Each assumes the one before it.

**Within Effective working: Lean before Uniform**

Lean eliminates waste first; Uniform then ensures consistency is the foundation that enables speed. Consistency applied to waste is still waste — Lean precedes.

---

## Alternatives considered

**Survival before Thinking** — rejected. Thinking is what produces systems that survive; reversing the chain breaks the causal argument.

**Effective working before Thinking** — rejected. Working lean on the wrong pattern is still waste. Thinking correctly must precede effective work.

**Transparent inside Thinking group** — rejected. Transparency is not a thinking practice; it is the condition under which all groups function. Placing it inside a group would misrepresent its scope.
